# ASR AI 🤖

ASR AI is an intelligent chatbot designed to assist you with a wide range of tasks. Whether you need to get the latest weather report, generate code snippets, find images, stay updated with news, perform web searches, define terms, tell a joke, or look up words in a dictionary, ASR AI has you covered. It's built with a user-friendly interface and offers 24/7 availability to cater to your needs anytime, anywhere.

## ✨ Key Features

ASR AI is packed with features to enhance your productivity and provide quick access to information.

*   **8 Versatile Modes:**
    *   **Weather ☁️:** Get up-to-date weather forecasts for any location.
    *   **Code 💻:** Generate code snippets for various programming languages.
    *   **Show Image 🖼️:** Find and display images based on your search queries.
    *   **News 📰:** Stay informed with the latest news from around the globe.
    *   **Web Search 🔍:** Perform quick and efficient web searches.
    *   **Define 📚:** Get clear and concise definitions of words.
    *   **Joke 😂:** Lighten your mood with a selection of humorous jokes.
    *   **Dictionary 📖:** Access a comprehensive dictionary for word meanings and usage.
*   **Toggle Button Theme 🎨:** Customize your visual experience with a light and dark mode toggle.
*   **Sidebar for Recent Chats 🕒:** Easily revisit your past conversations with a convenient sidebar.
*   **24/7 Availability 🕒:** ASR AI is always online and ready to assist you, day or night.

## 💡 How to Use

Using ASR AI is straightforward and intuitive.

1.  **Open the Link:** Navigate to the live demo at [https://asr-ai-omega.vercel.app/](https://asr-ai-omega.vercel.app/).
2.  **Accept Terms and Conditions:** Upon your first visit, you will be prompted to accept the terms and conditions. Please review and accept them to proceed.
3.  **Start Chatting:** Once accepted, you can begin interacting with ASR AI by typing your queries in the chat interface.

## 📦 Installation

ASR AI is a web-based application and does not require any local installation. You can access and use it directly through your web browser via the provided live demo link.
